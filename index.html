Why Compass Study

Built around how Ethiopian students actually study

Every part of the product answers to one question: does this help a student understand something they were stuck on?

📚

Curriculum-aligned, grade by grade

Lessons and practice quizzes mapped directly to the Ethiopian Grade 9–12 national curriculum, Natural and Social streams included.

💬

An AI coach that explains, not just answers

Ask a question in your own words and get a real explanation — not just the correct option, but why it's correct.

📱

Works on real devices, real networks

Once a lesson or quiz is generated, it's cached on the device — no repeated data cost, and it still works with no connection at all.

👥

Teachers and families, not just students

Teachers see class-wide progress. Parents see their own child's. Everyone gets exactly the visibility they need — nothing more.
